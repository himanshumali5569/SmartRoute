About SmartRoute

SmartRoute is a college attendance bus system.

SmartRoute helps students and drivers coordinate daily college bus travel. Students can mark whether they are coming, select their stop on the map, and view bus updates. Drivers can see student names, stop points, and manage pickup planning more clearly.

Purpose
Reduce confusion in daily bus attendance and make pickup planning simple.
System Name
SmartRoute
Students

Daily attendance

Students can record whether they are taking the bus on a specific day and save their pickup point.

Drivers

Clear route planning

Drivers can view which students are coming and where exactly they should stop.

Maps

Visual stop management

SmartRoute uses a map-based interface so pickup points are easier to understand than manual text lists.
